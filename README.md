# Binkit-Sales_Performance_dashboard
# Blinkit Sales & Delivery Performance Analytics (Power BI)

## 🎯 Project Business Objective
The goal of this analysis was to construct an interactive, multi-page business intelligence framework tracking **$32.19M in total revenue** and **$4.97M in core retail sales** for Blinkit (formerly Grofers). The dashboard provides stakeholders with immediate granularity into quick-commerce operational execution, consumer purchase behaviors, and delivery lifecycle constraints.

---

## 🛠️ Data Toolkit & Technical Architecture
* **Analytics Engine:** Power BI Desktop
* **Data Engineering & ETL:** Power Query (Automated structural data transformation, column restructuring, and schema sorting workflows).
* **Advanced Analytical Formulas:** Data Analysis Expressions (DAX) used to architect custom transactional variables, running balances, and time-intelligence parameters.

---

## 📊 Business Performance Metrics & Dynamic Analysis

### 1. Consumer Purchasing Behavioral Patterns
* **The Insight:** Engineered dedicated DAX algorithms isolating consumer transaction trends, revealing a massive **71.3% transaction bias toward weekends** compared to weekdays.
* **Actionable Impact:** Allows inventory supply routing and localized fulfillment hub staffing to be dynamically scaled up on Fridays through Sundays to minimize delivery latencies.

### 2. Category Performance & Revenue Concentration
* **The Insight:** Audited performance indicators across various inventory lines to discover that **Dairy products led all operational categories at $639K** in aggregate revenue volume.
* **Actionable Impact:** Isolates high-volume regional growth categories to better track active flash-marketing and partner hyper-local campaign.

### 3. Supply Chain & Delivery Fulfillment Auditing
* **The Insight:** Established real-time operational delivery parameters, separating statuses into **On-Time**, **Slightly Delayed**, and **Significantly Delayed**.
* **Actionable Impact:** Allows logistics managers to seamlessly isolate specific regional hubs or dark stores driving critical delays, preserving the brand's core ultra-fast delivery .



## 🖥️ Executive Dashboard Highlights
The analytics suite features dynamic, multi-view tabs built using UI/UX reporting best practices, including:
* **Interactive KPI Cards:** Instantly tracking macro-revenue, order density, and fulfillment baselines.
* **Drill-Down Matrixes:** Enabling business users to toggle between categorical sales segments and granular delivery performance variables.
* **Real-Time Sliders & Filtering:** Instantly filtering global data arrays by fulfillment timelines and store regions.
